## Design System Overview

ClaudeApp uses a **premium hybrid design system** combining:

### McLaren F1 Playbook
- **Papaya Orange** `#FF7300` - Primary brand color
- **Timing Curve** `cubic-bezier(0.19, 1, 0.22, 1)` - Precision animations
- **Anthracite** `#111314` - Technical dark surfaces

### Teenage Engineering EP-133
- **Warm Orange** `#FFC003` - Gradient accents
- **Light Typography** - 300 weight for elegance
- **LED Indicators** - Realistic glow effects
- **Calculator Aesthetic** - Monospaced data display

### KOSMA
- **Bracket Notation** `[TEXT]` - Section headers
- **Uppercase Tracking** - Technical labels
- **Bold Data Values** - Large percentages
- **Dark-First Design** - Deep black backgrounds

## Typography

- **Headlines**: Medium weight (500)
- **Labels**: Light weight (300)
- **Data Values**: Bold (700) monospaced
- **Tracking**: 1.2-2.0 for uppercase labels

---

## Animation

- **Standard**: 300ms with McLaren curve
- **Quick**: 200ms for micro-interactions
- **LED Pulse**: 800ms repeating for indicators
